About Us

Proposed system is a real time medical system useful for hospitals.

To predict reverse diabetes in diabetic patients using ML models.

This project proposes the development of a browser-based software application that leverages Machine Learning (ML) models such as K-Nearest Neighbour (KNN), Random Forest (RF) or Naïve Bayes, and Decision Tree to predict reverse diabetes in diabetic patients with higher accuracy.

  • To integrate Core medical factors in prediction (age, gender, sugar level, family history)..
  • To compare ML algorithms (KNN, RF, Naïve Bayes, Decision Tree) for best performance.
  • To provide a browser-based, role-specific hospital application.
